mylogreg.py and mylogre_reg.py 
===========

perform logistic regression and regularized logistic regression respectively and correspond to the solution of Problem Set #2 of Andrew Ng’s Machine Learning Class.

In mylogreg.py the aim is to predict whether a student gets admitted into a university, based on the applicant’s scores on two exams.

in mylog_reg.py the aim is to predict whether a microchip from a fabrication plant will pass quality assurance, based on the results of two tests.
